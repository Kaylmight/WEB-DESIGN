WAY TO EXTRATERRESTRIALS

China's giant alien-hunting radio telescope is finishing its testing-and-commissioning phase,
which has occurred over the past three (3) years.
Located in Southern China's Guizhou province,
The Five-hundred-meter Aperture Spherical Telescope (FAST) began these trial operations in September 2016.
Scientists are using FAST - the world's largest single-dish radio telescope - for a variety of tasks,
including to search for EXTRATERRESTRIAL INTELLIGENCE
